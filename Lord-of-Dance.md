# Lord of Dance

halo flame  
endless time  
cosmic dance  
death rebirth  

hour-glass drum  
sonic waves  
cosmic heart  
creation  

dwarf demon  
ignorance  
divine fire  
destruction  

matted hair  
sacred thread  
great river  
crescent moon  

tusker pose  
fear-not sign  
third-eye sight  
blinding light  

atom bits  
hadron CERN  
Chola bronze  
Lord Shiva  

_by Ravi Mynampaty_  
October 2022
